# Introduction

Subtraction undoes addition. Single-digit differences must be automatic
before borrowing makes sense.

## KP 1: Differences Within 10

### Worked Example

9 - 4: count back from 9 four times -> 8, 7, 6, 5. So 9 - 4 = 5.

### Problems

1. 8 - 3
2. 7 - 6
3. 6 - 2

## KP 2: Subtracting Back Through 10

### Worked Example

13 - 5: take 3 to reach 10, then 2 more -> 8. So 13 - 5 = 8.

### Problems

1. 12 - 4
2. 15 - 7
3. 11 - 8
