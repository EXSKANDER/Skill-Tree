# Introduction

Fluent single-digit sums are the atoms of all column arithmetic; they must
become automatic before multi-digit addition.

## KP 1: Sums Within 10

### Worked Example

4 + 3: count on from 4 three times -> 5, 6, 7. So 4 + 3 = 7.

### Problems

1. 5 + 2
2. 3 + 6
3. 4 + 4

## KP 2: Sums That Cross 10

### Worked Example

8 + 6: make ten first. 8 + 2 = 10, and 4 remain, so 8 + 6 = 14.

### Problems

1. 7 + 5
2. 9 + 6
3. 8 + 8
