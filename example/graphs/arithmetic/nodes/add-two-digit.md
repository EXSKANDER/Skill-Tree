# Introduction

Column addition combines place value with single-digit sums. Each knowledge
point adds one new wrinkle: no carrying, carrying to the tens, then carrying
into the hundreds.

## KP 1: Adding Two-Digit Numbers to Two-Digit Numbers

### Worked Example

32 + 45: add the ones (2 + 5 = 7), then the tens (3 + 4 = 7).

      32
    + 45
    ----
      77

### Problems

1. 21 + 34
2. 53 + 26
3. 40 + 37
4. 62 + 25

## KP 2: Adding Two-Digit Numbers to Two-Digit Numbers With Carrying

### Worked Example

47 + 38: ones give 7 + 8 = 15 -> write 5, carry 1 ten. Tens give
4 + 3 + 1 = 8.

      1
      47
    + 38
    ----
      85

### Problems

1. 36 + 27
2. 48 + 15
3. 29 + 56
4. 18 + 49

## KP 3: Adding Two-Digit Numbers to Two-Digit Numbers: Carrying to Hundreds

### Worked Example

85 + 67: ones give 5 + 7 = 12 -> write 2, carry 1. Tens give
8 + 6 + 1 = 15 -> write 5, carry 1 into the hundreds. Result: 152.

      11
      85
    + 67
    ----
     152

### Problems

1. 74 + 58
2. 96 + 45
3. 67 + 66
4. 89 + 34
